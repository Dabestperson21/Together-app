# Together

**A free, inclusive app for learning about bias, inclusion, and how to grow.**

Created by Bria Sharpton.

---

## What is Together?

Together is a safe, non-judgmental space where anyone can learn about unconscious bias, discrimination, and inclusion — through short videos, honest quizzes, games, and a searchable library of bias definitions.

There are no right or wrong answers. No shame. Just growth.

---

## Features

- **4 video lessons** — Implicit bias, the civil rights movement, microaggressions, and intersectionality
- **10 self-reflection quizzes** — Including a personality quiz, and reflections on racism, sexism, ableism, ageism, allyship, privilege, classism, homophobia, and religious bias
- **3 games, 50 levels each** — How Much Do You Know, Bias Investigator, and Ladder Up with per-level leaderboards
- **27 bias definitions** — Searchable library across racism, sexism, cognitive, and social categories
- **117 languages** — Change language instantly from the top of the app
- **9 accessibility settings** — High contrast, dyslexia-friendly font, large tap targets, reduced motion, no timer, letter spacing, plain language mode, screen reader support, and video captions

---

## Accessibility

Together is built for everyone. Accessibility and language settings are visible at the very top of the app before any navigation is required, so users with disabilities and non-English speakers can adjust the app immediately.

---

## Tech

Single-file HTML app — no frameworks, no dependencies, no backend. Just open `index.html` in any browser.
